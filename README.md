# NextBikeRouter

An iOS app designed to be used alongside the **NextBike** app to help users find the most efficient bike route to their destination. Automatically incorporates nearby **NextBike** stations and redirects to **Google Maps** for navigation.

---

## How to Use

1. **Install required apps**
   - [NextBike on App Store](https://apps.apple.com/se/app/nextbike/id504288371)
   - [Google Maps on App Store](https://apps.apple.com/se/app/google-maps/id585027354)

2. **Share a destination**
   - Open **Google Maps**.
   - Choose a destination.
   - Tap the **Share** button and select **NextBikeRouter** from the share sheet.

3. **Let NextBikeRouter calculate**
   - The app will determine the best route using nearby **NextBike** stations.

4. **Redirect to Google Maps**
   - NextBikeRouter will open **Google Maps** again, showing the full bike route including which bike stations to take.

5. **Follow the suggested route**
